CIS Scan Automation

CIS scanner is an internal scanner developed by AppSec team to assess the security posture of our GCP projects. This scanner scans a GCP project with Inspec GCP CIS Benchmark. Profiles listed below:

  • inspec-gcp-cis-benchmark
  • inspec-gke-cis-gcp
  • inspec-gke-cis-k8s

CIS scanner runs:

  • independent (weekly scanner)
  • on-demand (integrated to Sdarq)
